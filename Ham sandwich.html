Making a ham sandwich

To make a scrumptious ham sandwich, you will need the following food items:

  • Fresh bread
  • Ham
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise

To make your ham sandwich, proceed as follows:

  1. Take two slices of bread, and lay them flat on a cutting board.
  2. Spread some butter evenly on each slice.
  3. Place two slices of ham on the buttered bread.
  4. Spread some mayonnaise on the ham and/or on the other slice of buttered bread.
  5. Cut the bread, thus making two delicious sandwiches, and consume.
